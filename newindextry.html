Position Analysis

As we mentioned earlier, positions are more and more detailed in this sport, which has created certain patterns. First, size. As Olympic motto mentioned, "higher, faster, stronger". The body size of athletes are a key factor to measure the potential of a player. It is regretful to state that a 150 cm player cannot become a great football star in this sport with almost 100 percentage. He must make amazing efforts to be a professional player. Thus, for specific positions, players are more and more chosen on purpose for their size including their heights and weights. From the graph, we can see that most forwards appear to be around to cm tall, but the best appear to be around to cm. because many more high players have got scientific methods to level up their flexibility to win the battle with the similarly-sized defenders. And headers are great weapons for these tall players. For midfielders, the size requirement is far less strict than other positions, as they do not need too high speeds, flexibility is more valued because they can face challenges from every direction. Defenders' sizes are often similar to forwards, as we can see, many defenders are often high and strong, to help them fight against the forwards. And the goalkeeper. Most valuable goalkeepers are almost all high. If you review the games in the most recent World.Cup, you would have found out that most free kicks are not successful. Thanks to the science, more and more high male goalkeepers have gained the capabilities to cover almost half size of the goal, which is enough for free kick.
